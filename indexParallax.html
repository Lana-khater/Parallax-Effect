King of the Fjords, Sognefjord

Longest Fjord Adventure in Norway.

The Sognefjord is located in the northern part of the county of Vestland, in the middle of Western Norway.
From cruising the wildest and most beautiful arm of Sognefjord, the UNESCO-listed Nærøyfjord to visiting a charming village of Flåm located on the other arm of Sognefjord, Aurlandsfjord to riding the scenic Flåm Railway to driving one of the steepest roads in Northern Europe, Stalheimskleiva that offers the scenic stops like Stalheimsfossen and Sivlefossen waterfalls.

Galdhøpiggen

Norway's highest mountain

The Galdhøpiggen is Norway’s highest peak, and is simply breathtaking. The towering giant is the tallest mountain in Norway, as well as the highest in Northern Europe. When the sky is clear, you’ll have panoramic views of the surrounding Jotunheimen mountains.
